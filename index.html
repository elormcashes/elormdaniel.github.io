About Me

Hi, I’m Elorm Daniel, a Digital Forensics Investigator, Instructor, and Cybersecurity Specialist based in Accra, Ghana. I specialize in uncovering digital evidence, investigating cybercrime, and helping organizations secure their digital assets. Beyond investigations, I’m passionate about teaching and mentorship, guiding the next generation of cybersecurity professionals through training, awareness programs, and community engagement. Through initiatives like CyberExpose Podcast and Cyber Newbie Network, I share knowledge, expose scams, and make cybersecurity more practical and accessible. My mission is simple: to bridge the gap between technical expertise and real-world impact, one investigation and one learner at a time.
